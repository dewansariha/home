Definition

–A cheesy or corny joke that many people groan at or don't like. Bad Puns are often underestimated.
–A joke exploiting the different possible meanings of a word or the fact that there are words that sound alike but have different meanings.
–An operation in which an organ or tissue is moved or transfered.
